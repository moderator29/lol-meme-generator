Frequently asked

Answers to common questions

How do I track my parcel+

Enter your tracking number in the tracking card at the top of this page or inside the COURIO app to see live status updates.

What happens if my parcel is delayed+

You will receive an automatic notification with an updated delivery window, and our support team is available around the clock to help.

Do you offer insurance on shipments+

Yes, every shipment includes basic coverage, with optional extended insurance available at checkout for higher value items.

Can I integrate COURIO with my online store+

Our developer friendly API and plugins for popular platforms let you generate labels, calculate rates and track shipments automatically.

Which countries do you deliver to+

COURIO currently delivers to over sixty countries, with new regions added regularly as our network expands.
